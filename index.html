Food Menu

TAJINE

Tajine epitomizes a slow-cooking technique where ingredients are layered in a shallow, cone-shaped pot, allowing the flavors to meld together while gently simmering over low heat, resulting in tender, aromatic, and well-infused dishes

Price: 50dh

Harira with Chickpeas

typically prepared with tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, various herbs, and spices. It's often served during Ramadan to break the fast and is known for its hearty and nourishing qualities.

Price:60DH

COUSCOUS

Couscous consists of small, granular wheat particles that are steamed to perfection. Its mild flavor and fluffy texture make it an ideal base for absorbing aromatic spices and complementing a myriad of dishes, offering a delightful and versatile culinary experience.

Price:75DH

KABAB

Moroccan Kabab showcases the country's culinary prowess by combining tender, marinated meats with a fusion of spices, presenting a succulent grilled dish rich in North African heritage and characterized by its aromatic, nuanced flavors.

Price:45DH

LENTIL STEW

the lentil stew combines aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and paprika with lentils, tomatoes, vegetables, and sometimes meat, creating a rich, flavorful, and filling stew perfect for colder days.

Price:40DH

BRIOUAT

made with thin layers of pastry, typically stuffed with a savory filling like spiced minced meat (usually beef or chicken), almonds, herbs, and occasionally cheese or vegetables. It's folded into a triangular or cylinder shape, then fried until crispy and golden brown. Briouats are often served as appetizers or snacks in Moroccan cuisine

Price: 10DH
